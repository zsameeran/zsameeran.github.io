wisepotato Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23/07/2020

When you use our services, you’re trusting us with your information. We understand this is a big responsibility and work hard to protect your information and put you in control.

This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can update, manage, export, and delete your information.

We want you to understand the types of information we collect as you use our services.

Things you create or provide to us:

When you create a wisepotato Account, you provide us with personal information that includes your name, date of birth, email address, phone number, education details and a password. We also collect the content you create or receive from others when using our services. This includes things like messages you write and receive.

We collect information about your activity in our services, which we use to do things like recommend a skill relevant to your previously learnt skill. The activity information we collect may include:
• Skills you search.

We use data to build a bigger, stronger and better community.

We use the information we collect from all our users for the following purposes:

  • Measure performance

    We use data for analytics and measurement to understand how our services are used. For example, we analyze data about most desired skills and to check with the industry connect we have for providing better services in the demanded skill. We also use the data to study the demand trends and to ensure the smooth flow of the mentor allotment to the respective mentor requests. The data is also used to suggest the most demanded skill to the new users.

  • Communicate with you

    We use information we collect, like your email address, to interact with you directly. For example, we may send you a notification if we detect suspicious activity, like an attempt to sign in to your Skill Buddy Account from an unusual location. Or we may let you know about upcoming changes or improvements to our services. And if you contact us, we’ll keep a record of your request in order to help solve any issues you might be facing.

Managing, reviewing, and updating your information:


When you’re signed in, you can always review and update information by visiting the profile section of your account. The information such as Highest Degree can be updated with the respective proofs.

When this policy applies:

This Privacy Policy applies to all of the services offered by wisepotato. This Privacy Policy doesn’t apply to services that have separate privacy policies that do not incorporate this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy doesn’t apply to:

  • The information practices of other companies and organizations that advertise our services.
  • Services offered by other companies or individuals, including products or sites that may be advertised on this website (domain name.in)

Changes to this policy

We change this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your explicit consent. We always indicate the date the last changes were published and we offer access to archived versions for your review. If changes are significan t, we’ll provide a more prominent notice (including, for certain services, email notification of Privacy Policy changes).
